Gym Owner

    
 Gautam Mahanti (The Owner)

     Not all owners stay away from their gym all month long only to drop by at the end to collect their revenue. Gautam 
     Mahanti, the owner of Mission Fitness, is a welcome face almost every working day of the gym. From friendly banter, pointers here
     and there, to listening to everyone's fitness struggles and life stories: the man's smile is enough to light up your session. 
     A phenomenal bodybuilder himself, he stays involved in personal training sessions as well, bringing together his years of 
     experience and friendly nature to bring you a step closer to your goals everyday. A body as hard as a rock, and a heart 
     as soft as a feather: that's our Gautam Da for you.

Trainer

    
 Sourav Chand (The Trainer)

    The primary trainer and the greek-god of Mission Fitness, Sourav Chand, is an absolute beast. Starting with a physique
    which was painfully average, the man has, through his brutal training discipline and consistency of over 10 years, 
    transformed himself into a muscle monster. As a certified trainer and winner of multiple competitions, he holds deep 
    understanding of human anatomy, physiology, kinesiology and biomechanics. He is versed in sports nutrition, 
    competitive lifting and recovery and only follows proven workout plans tailored to everyone's personal needs. 
    Don't know where the 7.5kg dumbell is? Think your form might be off? Feeling a lack of energy over the past few 
    sessions? Sourav Da is your one stop solution for all your fitness related queries. When he is not in full trainer 
    mode, you will find him teasing literally every person that comes in front of his eyes, cleaning gym equipment or 
    scrolling through his spotify for the perfect music for the jukebox to fire up your session.

The Family

 The Incredible Family!

    At the heart of our gym is our incredible community of members who have evolved from strangers going to the same gym
    to family members who walk side by side on the road to fitness. Whether the day is a bright sunny one or a gloomy rainy one,
    Mission Fitness is always blooming with joy and vigour: attributes exuded by our members. From the 6 year old kid who is just
    starting his fitness journey to the elite powerlifter who is training for his next competition, our gym is a melting pot of 
    fitness enthusiasts from all walks of life. Pro tip: never miss gym on the day when it's someone's birthday- cuz they bring cake 
    for the entire gym !
